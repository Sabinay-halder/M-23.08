Height Tips

Diet for Increasing Height :

Include a lot of calcium, minerals, vitamins and proteins in your child’s diet. Proteins are the building blocks of our body. They help in cell development.

Include protein rich foods like fish, eggs, milk, legumes, etc in your child’s diet as proteins are essential ingredients to promote growth and height.

Other height increasing foods include: soya beans, chicken,milk, eggs, fruits, vegetables, oats, and dairy products.

Good nutrition and healthy lifestyle are important for proper development of child’s body. Sufficient rest and sleep is necessary. Children should be taught to develop good posture. Every child grows at his or her own pace, so don’t worry or feel too stressed.
