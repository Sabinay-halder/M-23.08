Weight Tips

Its provide high calorie foods other than going for chocolates, ice creams etc. So this post is the top 15 foods for weight gain in babies and kids.

1. Whole Cream Milk :

Whole Milk Cream can help your baby to put on weight. It is power packed with calcium and loaded with calories.

2. Whole Cream Yogurt :

Whole cream yogurt is an apt choice too.Please refrain from buying the flavour fruit yogurt available in the market as they are loaded with sugar. Instead just blend it in some fruits with the yogurt

3. Cheese :

Small piece of cheese as an evening snack can help here. Best option is home-made cottage paneer / cheese.

4. Banana :

Bananas are an excellent source of carbs and energy. Golden banana is excellent for weight gain.Please note to give one/half a banana everyday.

5. Avocado :

Avocado is rich in good fat. It is an excellent food for weight gain too. Just blend it in with whole cream milk or plain mashed .

6. Sweet Potato :

Sweet Potato again is rich in sugar and beta carotene . You can give it in the form of sweet potato mash with cheese and whole cream milk.

7. Eggs :

Eggs are power packed with protein. Yellow yolk could be started from 8 Months and whole eggs from 1 year.

8. Potato :

Potato can help to put on weight . Excellent source of carbohydrates and energy, these can be given in the form Potato cheese mash.

9. Ghee :

The same golden rule above applies for ghee as well.

11. Peanut Butter :

Peanut butter is an excellent source for weight gain. If your baby is 1 year +, you can spread a teaspoon of peanut butter on a bread / toast and give it to your child.

12. Olive Oil :

Olive oil contains good fat. It would be wise if you start preparing baby food in olive oil.

14. Nuts :

Mixed nuts are enriched with Vitamin E, proteins and fat. You can refer to the home made mixed dry fruits powder and chocolate almond powder for babies and toddlers.
